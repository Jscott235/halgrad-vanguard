Quick Rules
Armoury
Progression
Turn Order
0
Deployment
Place Denizens first. Players roll off, then alternate deploying units starting with the loser of the roll.
1
Initiative Phase
Both players roll D10. Winner chooses who goes first for the entire turn. Ties re-roll.
2
Movement Phase
Active player moves all units up to their Sp value. Charging adds +2". Then next player moves. Denizens move last.

Charging: Must end in base contact by shortest route. Charged unit may Fall Back (half move, triggers Opportunity Attacks on 6+).
Threat Radius: 1" bubble — enemies can't enter without Charging the nearest unit.
Climb: D10: 1–3 fall prone, 4–7 climb but stop, 8–10 climb and continue.
Actions (one per unit): Minor = half move, no Ranged. Major = half move, no further move or Ranged.
3
Shoot Phase
Active player shoots with all ranged weapons. Roll D10 vs unit's Ra score to hit. Then roll D10 + ranged S vs target R to wound.

Cover saves: Woods/Friendly = 6+, Ruins = 5+, Fortifications = 4+
If moved: Worsen Ra by 1 (e.g. 6+ becomes 7+)
Cannot shoot through friendlies into combat involving friendlies.
4
Fight Phase
All units in base contact must fight. Priority player chooses order. Both sides roll D10 + Me. Winner makes Strike rolls (D10 + S vs R). On success = Wound.

Natural 10: Auto-win. Natural 1: Auto-lose.
Cornered: Enemy doubles their attacks. Critical Hit: Wounds on 5+ (not Great Weapons).
Losing side: Falls back 1" unless Cornered (no space to fall back).
5
Resolution Phase
Check if each Warband is Broken (half starting models lost). Broken warbands must pass Nerve Tests or units flee. Warband Routed when all remaining units fail.
✕
End Phase (Post-Game)
Calculate Victory Points. Each player gains 1D10×3 GP. Roll for Injuries. Calculate & spend Experience. Purchase Equipment and Reinforcements.

Progression Routes
Warriors → Level up → Gesture / Promotion / Destined for Greatness
Promoted Warriors → become the next tier unit in their faction (e.g. Man At Arms → Sworn Sword)
Destined for Greatness → become a Character with 1 Destiny + Skill Points
Characters → gain Skill Points → unlock Specialism Traits → can become Leader or Second
Seconds → automatically become Leader if current Leader is slain
Note: Monsters cannot gain XP, become Characters, or complete Searches.
